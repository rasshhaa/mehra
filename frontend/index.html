THE EXISTING GAP

Why AutoVault had to exist

The vehicle ecosystem in the UAE runs on disconnected platforms. Owners, garages, insurers, RTA and Tasjeel each maintain isolated systems — leading to redundant data entry, processing delays, limited transparency and a higher risk of fraud and human error.

01

Fragmented Vehicle Data

No single source of truth

Vehicle data is scattered across RTA, insurers, garages and marketplaces — making it impossible to access a complete and trusted vehicle history in one place. Buyers, garages and insurers can't reliably verify past accidents, inspections or ownership.

02

Disconnected Stakeholder Workflows

Silos across every step

Inspection, insurance approval, ownership transfer and repair operate in silos. Each stakeholder independently collects and verifies the same data — causing duplication, delays and inefficient end-to-end workflows.

03

Manual & Error-Prone Verification

Paper, phone calls, repeat checks

Critical documents — insurance certificates, inspection reports, ownership records — are often verified manually or through disconnected systems, increasing the risk of human error, fraud, invalid records and processing delays.

Existing Gap

There is no unified, multi-stakeholder digital platform for end-to-end vehicle lifecycle management in the UAE. AutoVault closes that gap.

WHAT AutoVault DELIVERS

Eight services. One platform.
Every stakeholder in sync.

Each AutoVault service replaces a fragmented step in the UAE vehicle lifecycle and connects the owner, the garage, the insurer and the authorities through a single digital flow. Tap any card to see how it links the ecosystem together.
